My view on travelling

"The best part about travelling is when you just tag along." - Ricci

Around Brazil

I've been to many places around Brazil. For those that missed Geography 101, Brazil has 5 main regions and 26 states plus a Federal District. If you open up a map, you'll see a very small square spot at the very center. That's the Federal District, and that's where I live. Feel free to wave hello.
Other than that, I've been to 15 other states and 4 of the 5 regions. The only one left is the North region (that's where the Amazon Rainforest is) and the reason for that is: 1. I hate the combination heat+humidity and 2. Mosquitos.

Here are some pictures. There aren't many because I'm a more "appreciate the experience" kind of guy and I forget to take them.
